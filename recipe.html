Quinoa Salad

Recipe for a super enhanced and flavorful quinoa salad, made with paprika, carrots, tomatoes, lettuce, cucumber, and olive oil.

10min Easy 1-2 servings

Ingredients

  • 250g of quinoa grains
  • Half a teaspoon of paprika
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Half a tablespoon of grated ginger
  • 1 grated carrot
  • 1 tomato, peeled and seedless, diced
  • 1 tablespoon of chopped green onions
  • 1 cucumber
